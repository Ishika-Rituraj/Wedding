By Air

Nearest Airport

Fly into Maharana Pratap Airport, Udaipur (UDR) — approximately 100 km from the resort, around 1 hr 45 min by road. Well connected from Mumbai, Delhi, Bengaluru, and more. We recommend arriving 16–17 December.

🚗

By Road

Driving & Transfers

Kumbhalgarh is ~2 hours from Udaipur and ~3.5 hours from Jaipur by road. The resort offers airport transfer services — contact the hotel to arrange. Complimentary on-site parking available. Taxis and car hire available from Udaipur airport.

🗺️

Explore

Nearby Attractions

Kumbhalgarh Fort is just 3.3 km away — a UNESCO World Heritage Site. The Kumbhalgarh Wildlife Sanctuary is steps from the resort. Ranakpur Jain Temple and Udaipur's City Palace are within easy reach.
